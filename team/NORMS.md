Everyone comes to meetings on time

Absences are communicated in advance and work is made up according to the meeting notes

Standups are short and to the point

Each team members will work on their branch and require at least one team member to approve the pull

We will address issues and conflicts in a respectful manner either through edstem, video call, or in person meetings

The group will meet 3 times a week. Sprints will last 2 weeks. Standup meetings will occur at the start of every meeting. Friday meetings will include standups, sprint reviews (showing the TA's our work) and retrospective.
