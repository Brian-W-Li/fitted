# Problem Scenario

A UCSB student often struggles to decide what to wear for class or social events.
They want an outfit that matches the weather, the occasion, and their personal style,
but finding the right combination takes time and causes unnecessary stress.

Current solutions, such as asking friends or using generic fashion apps, are inconsistent
and do not provide personalized or context-aware recommendations.

The student wishes for a simple way to receive outfit suggestions that help them feel
confident and prepared for the day.
