I'm a 4th year computer science major from San Jose, CA interested in building scalable and user-focused applications. 

## Project Ideas & Interests

For our project, I’m interested in building an app that helps users decide what to wear using items from their own wardrobe. Many people struggle with daily outfit decisions, and current solutions are either manual or not personalized enough.

Key ideas I’m excited about:
- Allowing users to upload and categorize their clothing items
- Automatically generating complete outfits by mixing and matching pieces
- Letting users lock favorite items or rate outfits so the app learns preferences

For the tech-stack:
- React of Next.js for the frontend
- Python or Node.js for the backend
- PostgreSQL vs NoSQL database (?)
- Potential machine learning for personalized outfit suggestions

(These are just ideas, always open to change)
