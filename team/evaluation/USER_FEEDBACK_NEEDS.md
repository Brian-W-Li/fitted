# User Feedback Needs

We’d love feedback on the following three areas. Each has a metric that is testable by the team

## 1. Recommendation relevance

Do the recommended outfits feel relevant and useful and do users see at least one suggestion they’d actually wear?

**Metric:**  
- **Target:** More than **50%** of test users rate relevance of at least **7 or 8** (out of 10).  
- **Testable:** After the user sees a set of recommendations (any occasion), ask:  
  “How relevant were these outfit recommendations to you?”  
  **1 = Not at all and 10 = Very relevant**

**How the feedback team can measure it:**  
Run the app, request recommendations once (e.g. for “Casual”), then rate it 1–10  question. We count the share of responses that are 7 or 8.

## 2. Occasion fit

When users pick an occasion (e.g. Business or Formal), do the results match what they expect for that context?

**Metric:**  
- **Target:** More than **50%** of test users **agree** (or strongly agree) that recommendations matched the selected occasion.  
- **Testable:** User selects an occasion (e.g. “Business”), gets recommendations, then is asked:  
  “The recommendations matched the occasion I selected (e.g. business-appropriate).”  
  **Strongly disagree | Disagree | Neutral | Agree | Strongly agree**

**How the feedback team can measure it:**  
Pick one occasion (e.g. Business or Formal), request recommendations, then answer the agree/disagree question. We count “Agree” + “Strongly agree” as success.

## 3. Add-item flow: ease and speed

Is the flow from upload -> CV analysis -> form clear and not frustrating, and how long does it take from uploading a photo to the CV actually running and the form being pre-filled? We care about both perceived ease and actual wait time.

**Metric:**  
- **Ease:** Average “ease” score **≥ 4.0 / 5** and **≥ 90%** of test users successfully add at least one item.  
- **Speed:** **Median time ≤ 15 seconds** from “image selected / Analyze triggered” to “CV results visible (form pre-filled)”. At least **90%** of attempts complete within **30 seconds**.

**Testable (one task, three things to record):**  
Ask the user to add one clothing item (upload a photo).

1. **Time:** Start a stopwatch when they trigger the analyze step (e.g. click “Analyze” or “Next” after upload). Stop when the form appears with CV-filled fields (category, type, colors). Record time in seconds.  
2. **Completed?** Did they finish adding the item (saved to wardrobe)? Yes/No.  
3. **Ease:** After they finish (or give up), ask: *“How easy was it to add this item (from upload to saving)?”* **1 = Very difficult … 5 = Very easy**

**How the feedback team can measure it:**  
Do the “add one item” task once. Report: (a) time in seconds from Analyze to form pre-filled, (b) whether you completed the add, (c) your 1–5 ease rating. Repeating 2–3 times (e.g. different images) gives us a median time. We aggregate ease score, completion rate, and time percentiles.
