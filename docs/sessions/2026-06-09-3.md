## Decisions / why
- **Two-stage cache** (cache the GPT-candidate stage on `session_seed` inputs; re-rank
  per-request with `tiebreak_seed(generationIndex)`) chosen over "add generationIndex to the
  cache key" — keeps R1's cache≡seed invariant intact and still makes re-roll cheap+different.
- **Integer half-up rounding** over `round()` — Python banker's rounding splits the real caps in
  opposite directions (35→24, 25→18) and wouldn't survive a TS/numpy reimpl at the M5/M6
  boundary.
- Verified the two strongest blockers myself rather than trusting the agent: the `\x1f`
  collision and the SlotMap count (it's **2** undetectable cases, not 3 — first agent
  overcounted).
