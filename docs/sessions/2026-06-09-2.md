## Decisions / why
- **Read B over Read A.** Keeps the ML-depth dive (the whole point per the fork's framing) — v1.2 builds the clean substrate, and the trained model plugs into M1's 30% signal slot at M6. The spec's heuristic personalization is the v1 placeholder, not the ceiling.
- **Python `ml-system/fitted_core/`.** The M6 model is Python, the legacy `_score_outfit` seam is Python, and M0/M1 are pure functions that unit-test cleanly with no Mongo/keys. The TS app consumes the substrate at M5 via a `fetch()` service call, not a code port.
- **Fly.io, not HF Spaces.** No cold starts (run an always-on machine; HF free-tier cold-starts bit the team in CS 148), no teammate IP boundary, no constraint on the M6 model type (classical or small-DL). Note: Fly.io's free tier was discontinued (Oct 2024), so always-on is a small paid cost (~$2–5/mo), not free — the cold-start argument stands, the "free" framing does not.
